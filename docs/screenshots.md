# Screenshots

A quick visual tour of CheckCheck across desktop and mobile, in both light and
dark themes.

## Desktop

Colour-coded cards on a board, pinned cards up top, labels in the sidebar, and
a search bar for finding things fast.

### Dark theme

## Card editor

Open a card to edit its items, reorder them by drag and drop, and add notes.
Card notes support Markdown.
